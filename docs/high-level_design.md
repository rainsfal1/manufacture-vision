Executive Summary

This project develops a real-time situational awareness platform for manufacturing environments to improve worker safety, regulatory compliance, perimeter security, and incident response. The system ingests live CCTV/RTSP or VMS/NVR video streams, performs edge-based perception and event detection, and produces actionable alerts, evidence clips, and operational analytics. Rather than replacing existing security or plant systems, it functions as an intelligence layer that augments monitoring, reduces manual oversight, and provides audit-ready reporting for safety and compliance teams. The platform is designed for on-prem, edge-capable deployment with optional industrial integrations (e.g., MQTT/webhooks) and supports phased adoption through independent yet interoperable modules.



Core Modules

PPE Compliance Monitoring

Detects required protective equipment (e.g., helmets, safety vests, gloves) by zone and shift policy. Generates real-time alerts and compliance logs to support safety enforcement and regulatory audits.

Workplace Safety

Identifies unsafe behaviors and hazardous conditions using zone-aware detection and tracking, including restricted-area entry, proximity to dangerous equipment, and unsafe activity in designated work zones. Produces incident events and trend metrics to reduce risk and prevent accidents.

Equipment & Machine Monitoring

Monitors machine areas and operational states via video analytics to detect irregular activity patterns, unauthorized usage, and abnormal stoppage/idle behavior. Generates operational event logs that support rapid response and downtime reduction.

Assembly Line Optimization

Measures station activity and flow patterns to identify bottlenecks, delays, and idle time across production lines. Provides throughput-oriented analytics and event logs that support process improvement and efficiency gains.

Intrusion & Perimeter Security

Detects unauthorized access, perimeter breaches, and after-hours presence using zone rules, tripwires, and tracking persistence. Produces security alerts and evidence clips for rapid response and incident investigation.

Fire & Smoke Detection

Detects early indicators of fire and smoke in monitored areas and triggers real-time alerts with evidence capture. Supports faster emergency response and reduces damage and operational disruption.

This platform establishes a unified perception, policy, and event-processing foundation that enables continuous awareness, operational clarity, and modular expansion across manufacturing safety, security, and productivity workflows.
